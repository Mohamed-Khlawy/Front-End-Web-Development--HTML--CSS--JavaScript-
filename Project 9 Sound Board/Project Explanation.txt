In this project we did a Sound Borad with 6 different Quranic surah. Let's explain it.

In our web page we have 6 Buttons, each button of them have a text of the name of the Quranic surah that will play when you press this button.

When you press any button of them the Quranic surah will start to play and you will hear it clearly from your browser.

If you press another button, the Previous surah will stop to start the one you press now.

Now, I will let you to try this beautiful project and hear these 6 beautiful Quranic surahs, by clicking on the button with the surah that you want to hear.
